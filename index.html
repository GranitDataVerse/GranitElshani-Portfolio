My experience
Hello, my name is Granit Elshani, and I'm a highly motivated data-driven professional with a Master's Degree in Banking and Finance. I recently completed the Google Data Analytics course on Coursera, enhancing my skills in data analysis tools like SQL, SPSS, Python, and visualization tools.

As a Data Quality Analyst at Ipsos Market Research in Europe, Kosovo, I played a pivotal role in ensuring the accuracy and reliability of data used in critical decision-making processes. I developed and implemented robust data quality practices, which significantly improved data accuracy and consistency. By creating reusable formulas for data extraction and transformation, I streamlined performance analysis, leading to faster insights generation and improved project turnaround times.

Additionally, I served as an Assistant Project Manager at Ipsos, where I led initiatives that enhanced project management efficiency through the use of predictive analytics and data-driven insights. This resulted in improved project outcomes and client satisfaction for high-profile clients including the European Union, Ministry of Justice in Kosovo, Raiffeisen Bank, European Bank for Reconstruction and Development, British Tobacco, Philip Morris, and Coca-Cola in Kosovo.

My experience also includes roles as a Database Administrator and Field Manager, where I developed and optimized databases for research projects, managed and trained field interviewers, and implemented data collection protocols to ensure compliance with research standards.

Outside of work, in my free time, I enjoy working with data, discovering patterns, and sharing insights through various projects, which helps me stay updated with the latest trends in data analysis.

When I'm not working on a dataset, you can find me running or biking on trails.

I hope you enjoy exploring the projects in my portfolio, which showcase my passion for data analysis and management.
